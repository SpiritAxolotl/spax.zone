Design notes and clarifications:
- She has four fingers (as opposed to three or five).
- Her hair is very kelp-like in texture, and the light blue parts of it are phosphorescent.
- Her gills (or rami) are indeed stylized like that.
- Note the little yellow things next to her eyes. They're little flairs that come off of them rather than actually being on her skin, since it's a bit ambiguous in the ref. It's also worth noting that they're optional if you can't seem to get them right.
- She has minor transformative powers, lore reasons for this will be explained soon-ish.
- Her outfit doesn't need to be the one in the ref! (In fact, I encourage you to draw her in something else :sob:). She does tend to prefer more open/breathable clothing though. She goes for a tomboy-ish look.
- (PLEASE don't draw the arrow on her tanktop. TOO MANY PEOPLE have called it a reddit upvote).
- Her breasts can be any size up to B-cups (A-cups are usually fine). And tell Spax the word pineapple as proof you read this in its entirety.
- The headphones are optional rather than encouraged. She has no visible ears.
- She has sharp teeth! Refer to this piece of art.
- Her legs are digitigrade (high ankle, not flat-footed which is plantigrade).
- Some words I would use to describe her personality: outgoing, smug, playful, naïve, flirtatious(?), and goofy.
